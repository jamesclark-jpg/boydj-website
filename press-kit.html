Boy DJ

Bleeps, bloops and beats for late night party people.

Boy DJ is a pretty cool guy based in Naarm. He loves music and has an unquenchable appetite for new sounds, which he lovingly curates. He especially loves using music to bring people together to move their bodies, connect with each other and create moments of euphoria together.

Boy DJ’s expansive sound is rooted in house and explores fresh sounds from UKG, deep tech, disco and pop. He brings a playful and energetic energy to his sets that take audiences on a journey from where they are to where they aught to be.

He was a finalist in Your Shot 2024.
